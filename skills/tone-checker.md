# Tone Checker

## Purpose

Review content for tone and voice consistency against established guidelines.

## Checklist

- [ ] Voice is active, not passive
- [ ] Sentences are concise and scannable
- [ ] Jargon is avoided or explained
- [ ] Tone matches the target audience
- [ ] CTAs are clear and actionable
- [ ] Headings are descriptive and parallel in structure

## Tone Guidelines

| Attribute   | Do                          | Don't                        |
|-------------|-----------------------------|------------------------------|
| Clarity     | Use plain language          | Use unnecessary jargon       |
| Confidence  | Be direct and helpful       | Be vague or wishy-washy      |
| Warmth      | Be conversational           | Be robotic or overly formal  |
| Brevity     | Get to the point            | Pad with filler words        |

## How to Use

When reviewing content, run through the checklist and tone guidelines above. Flag any items that don't align and suggest specific rewrites.
